Critical messaging rule

Never say: “We use only local materials.”

That creates risk. It sounds like quality may be compromised just to stay local.

Say this instead: “We prioritize Northeast-sourced materials wherever they are efficient, reliable, and project-suitable.”

Financial logic12 / 14
What to avoid

Mistakes that can weaken the model.

Avoid

  • Claiming “only local materials”
  • Building a complicated app too early
  • Selling as the cheapest construction option
  • Depending only on Instagram for leads
  • Giving updates without proof or timestamps

Do instead

  • Say “Northeast-prioritized sourcing where suitable”
  • Start with a clean MVP dashboard
  • Sell trust, climate-fit, and execution quality
  • Use partnerships and referrals
  • Create evidence-based progress records
